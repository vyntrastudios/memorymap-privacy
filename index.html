Privacy Policy – MemoryMap

Effective Date: March 2026

MemoryMap values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address for authentication.
  • Photos and media that you choose to upload.
  • Location data to allow you to save and explore memories on the map.
  • Basic device and usage information to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality.
  • To securely store and synchronize your memories using cloud services.
  • To improve user experience and fix technical issues.
  • To protect accounts and prevent misuse.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication and Cloud Storage (Google)
  • OpenStreetMap services for map display

4. Permissions

  • Camera – to take photos for memories
  • Photos / Media – to upload selected images
  • Location – to display your position and save locations

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Delete your account
  • Request data removal
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns
